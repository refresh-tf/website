About Refresh!

“Refresh” is a project dedicated to updating existing TF2 maps to meet modern competitive standards. Drawing on our experience, we've addressed gameplay issues through geometry fixes, while also making subtle aesthetic and performance improvements. We invite you to try these updated maps and share your feedback, so we can continue improving the quality of the competitive map pool together.
